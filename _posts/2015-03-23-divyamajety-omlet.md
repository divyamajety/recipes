### Ingredients for Omlet 

1. 2 eggs
2. 1 onion
3. 1 tomato
4. pinch of salt
5. pepper
6. few leafs spinach
7. few leafs coriander

### Instructions
Combine eggs with onion,tomato,salt,pepper,spinach,coriander and mix well.
Pour the liquid on the hotpan brushed with olive oil.
After 1min turn it to other side.
Serve in the plate.
