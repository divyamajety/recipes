# Divya's omlet

1. 2 eggs
2. 1 onion
3. 1 tomato
4. pinch of salt
5. pepper
6. few leafs spinach
7. few leafs coriander

Combine eggs with onion,tomato,salt,pepper,spinach and coriander.
mix well.
pour the liquid on the hotpan brushed with olive oil.
after 1min turn it to other side.
serve in the plate.
